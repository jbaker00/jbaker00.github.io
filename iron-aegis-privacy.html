Privacy Policy

Iron Aegis — Classic Arcade City Defense

Effective Date: May 2, 2025

Iron Aegis does not collect, store, or share any personal information directly. The app is free to play and supported by advertising through Google AdMob.

Overview

Iron Aegis ("the App") is an arcade city-defense game developed by James Baker. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use it on your iPhone or iPad.

Data We Do NOT Collect

Iron Aegis does not directly collect any of the following:

  • Names, email addresses, or any account information
  • Location data
  • Photos, contacts, or files
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to our own servers

Advertising — Google AdMob

The App is free and supported by advertising. We use Google AdMob to display banner ads and full-screen interstitial ads. As part of delivering ads, Google AdMob may collect and use certain information from your device, including:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA) — subject to your App Tracking Transparency choice on iOS 14.5 and later
  • IP address and general location (country/region level)
  • Device type, operating system version, and language
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with their policies. For more information, please review:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google AdMob uses data

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time through your iOS device settings under Privacy & Security → Tracking. When you deny tracking, non-personalized ads are shown instead.

Data Storage

All game data — including scores and progress — is stored exclusively on your device. Nothing is transmitted to our own servers.

Children's Privacy

The App is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. All advertising is delivered subject to Google AdMob's policies for apps directed to children. If you are a parent or guardian with concerns, please contact us using the information below.

Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the new version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date. Significant changes will also be noted in the App Store release notes.
