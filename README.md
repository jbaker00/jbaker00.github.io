# James Baker - Multi-Platform Developer Portfolio

Personal portfolio website showcasing apps and projects across iOS, Android,
Roku, macOS, Linux, and the web.

## Highlights

- **Creole Translator** (iOS / Android) — AI-powered voice translation from Haitian Creole to English
- **ResortBrowser** — Ski resort explorer with real-time conditions
- **Suburban NJ - NYC Scheduler** — NJ Transit schedule and trip planner
- **DashSaver / LiveBoard** — Roku screensaver & channel with live clocks, weather, and stocks
- **TeleMix** — Roku channel streaming Haiti TV & music 24/7
- **JamaicaDeals**, **MauiTrolly** — Travel apps
- **AAC** — iPad/iPhone AAC app for non-verbal users with Groq/OpenAI TTS
- **GoogleAdMobMonitor**, **Dev Dashboard** — Developer analytics & revenue tracking
- See the full grid on the live site for everything else.
