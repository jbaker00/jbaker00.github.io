Support

Global Vibes — Travel Documents & AI Trip Assistant

Need help with Global Vibes? Browse the common questions below or reach out directly — we typically respond within 1–2 business days.

Accounts & Sign-In

How do I get an account?

Global Vibes accounts are created by your travel agent at Global Vibes Travel. If you booked a trip and don't have login details yet, contact your agent at info@globalvibestravel.com.

I see "This account is not registered" when signing in with Google.

Your Google email must first be registered by your travel agent. Ask your agent to add the exact email address you use with Google Sign-In, then try again.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

On the sign-in screen, tap Forgot Password and enter your email — a reset link will be sent to you. If you sign in with Google, your password is managed by Google, not the app.

How do I change my password?

After signing in, tap the profile icon in the top right and choose Change Password. This option only appears for email/password accounts.

Documents

Where do my documents come from?

Your travel agent uploads your booking confirmations, itineraries, insurance, and other trip documents to your portal. If something is missing, contact your agent.

How do I view a document?

Tap the eye icon on any document card to open a full preview. PDFs and images open right in the app.

Can I filter my documents?

Yes — when you have documents with different labels (Flight, Hotel, Insurance, etc.), filter chips appear above the list. Tap one to filter.

AI Features

What does "Generate" trip summary do?

It uses AI to create a friendly overview of your upcoming trip from the documents in your portal — one tap and you get your trip at a glance.

What can I ask the AI chat?

Tap Ask AI and ask anything about your trip or destination — what to pack, local tips, weather expectations, and more.

The AI says I've reached a limit.

AI features have fair-use limits to keep the service fast for everyone. Wait a little while and try again.

Ads & Privacy

Why do I see ads?

Ads keep the app free for Global Vibes Travel clients. You can choose whether ads are personalized when iOS asks for tracking permission — declining still shows ads, just non-personalized ones.

How do I delete my account or data?

Email info@globalvibestravel.com from your registered address and request deletion. Your account and documents will be removed.

Contact Support

If your issue isn't covered above, please reach out and include:

  • Your iPhone model and iOS version (found in Settings → General → About)
  • The email address on your account
  • A description of what you expected to happen vs. what actually happened
