Privacy Policy

Country Road Tower Defense

Effective Date: June 21, 2026

Country Road Tower Defense collects no personal data whatsoever. No account is required to play.

Overview

Country Road Tower Defense ("the App") is an iOS tower defense game developed by James Baker. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App processes and how it is handled.

Data We Do NOT Collect

The developer does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any account credentials
  • No account or login is required to play
  • No analytics or crash reporting is collected by the developer
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Your contacts or address book
  • Clipboard contents
  • Device identifiers or IP addresses

Advertising

The App displays banner advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier and other signals to serve contextual or personalized ads. You can limit ad tracking by going to Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising on your device and toggling off personalized ads. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

Children's Privacy

Country Road Tower Defense is not directed at children under the age of 13. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the new version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.
