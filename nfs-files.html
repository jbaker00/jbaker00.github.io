NFS Files

The fast, focused NFS client for iPhone and iPad. Your NAS, one tap away.

Coming soon to the App Store

Built for NFS, not buried in menus. Most file managers treat NFS as one checkbox among twenty. NFS Files does one thing well: it finds your servers, shows your shares, and gets out of the way.

What it does

Finds your NAS

NFS servers on your network appear as you type — no hunting for IP addresses.

Lists your shares

Tap "Find Shared Folders" and the app asks your server for its export list. Never guess /volume1/media again.

Browse fast

Folders first, sort by name, date, or size, instant search, and a live free-space readout.

Preview everything

Photos, video, PDFs, and documents open right in the app, with the system share sheet one tap away.

Transfer both ways

Download with progress, upload files and photos, rename, delete, and create folders.

Live status

Green dots show which servers are reachable right now, before you even tap.

Simple, honest protocol support. NFS Files speaks NFSv3 — the version every NAS supports out of the box. No Kerberos, no domain, no accounts to manage: your server decides who connects by network address, and your files never leave your network.

Works with Synology, QNAP, TrueNAS, UGREEN, Unraid, Raspberry Pi, Linux, and macOS.
