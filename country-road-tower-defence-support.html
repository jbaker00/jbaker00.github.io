Placing Towers

How do I place a tower?

Tap any white circle on the map — those are cousin houses. A build menu will appear at the bottom. Choose Aunt Emma's Fireworks ($50) or Aunt Junita's Coke Cannon ($100). Towers take a few seconds to build before they start firing.

I tapped a circle but nothing happened.

The circle may already have a tower. Check if it's occupied — the build menu will say "Tower already placed." Also make sure you have enough coins to afford the tower you want.

Waves & Enemies

How do I start a wave?

Tap the green Start Wave button in the top-right corner. Use the time before each wave to place towers strategically across both roads.

I can't afford a tower. What do I do?

Kill more enemies in the current wave to earn coins. The Go-Kart Bandit drops 10 coins when defeated; the Garbage Truck drops 20 coins.

What's the difference between the two towers?

Aunt Emma's Fireworks ($50) fires fast with light damage — great for slowing swarms of Go-Karts. Aunt Junita's Coke Cannon ($100) fires slower but hits hard — essential for stopping armored Garbage Trucks.

Why are enemies taking two different paths?

The compound has two roads. Some enemies go up the left side past Cheryl, Toi, and Jimmy, then sweep right across the top. Others sweep right along the bottom past John, Lamar, Santhia, and Oliver Jr., then climb the right side through Kim, Oliver G., Lendl, and Carolyn. You need to cover both routes!

Troubleshooting

The game froze or crashed.

Force-close the app (swipe up from the bottom on newer iPhones, or double-press the home button on older ones, then swipe the app away) and reopen it. If the problem keeps happening, please email support with your device model and iOS version.
