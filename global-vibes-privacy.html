Privacy Policy

Global Vibes — Travel Documents & AI Trip Assistant

Effective date: July 4, 2026

Global Vibes is the client app for Global Vibes Travel. Your travel documents are private — they are visible only to you and your travel agent, and we never sell your personal data.

Who We Are

The Global Vibes app is operated by Global Vibes Travel. You can reach us at info@globalvibestravel.com.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: your name and email address, provided by your travel agent when your account is created (or by Google when you use Google Sign-In).
  • Travel documents: booking confirmations, itineraries, insurance policies, and similar files that your travel agent uploads for you.
  • Authentication data: handled by Google Firebase Authentication. If you sign in with Google, Google's own privacy policy also applies.
  • Advertising identifier (IDFA): only if you grant permission through the iOS App Tracking Transparency prompt (see "Advertising" below).

How Your Data Is Stored

Your account details and documents are stored in Google Firebase (Firestore and Cloud Storage). Data is encrypted in transit, and access is restricted by security rules so that only you and your travel agent can view your documents.

Advertising

The app shows ads served by Google AdMob, which keeps the app free. When you first launch the app, iOS asks whether the app may track you:

  • If you allow tracking, the advertising identifier (IDFA) may be used to show more relevant, personalized ads.
  • If you decline, you still see ads — just non-personalized ones.

You can change this choice anytime in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking. Learn how Google uses information from apps that use its services at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

AI Features

When you generate a trip summary, ask the AI assistant a question, or your agent checks document completeness, document names and labels (not full file contents) may be sent to an AI service to produce the response. This content is used only to answer your request and is not used to train AI models.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is shared only with the service providers needed to run the app — Google Firebase (storage, authentication), Google AdMob (advertising), and our AI provider (AI features) — and only to the extent required to provide the service.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your account and documents are kept while you are a client of Global Vibes Travel. To request deletion of your account and all associated data, email info@globalvibestravel.com from your registered address — we will remove your data promptly.

Children's Privacy

Global Vibes is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update this page and revise the effective date above.
