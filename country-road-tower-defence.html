Country Road Tower Defense

Defend the Wilkerson Compound!


Download on the App Store

Protect the Big House

🛣️

Two Enemy Routes

Enemies charge in from two directions — up the left side and sweeping across the bottom. You must defend both roads at once.

🎆

Aunt Emma's Fireworks

Fast-firing fireworks tower that showers Go-Kart Bandits with rapid explosions. Affordable at $50 and great for slowing down swarms.

🥤

Aunt Junita's Coke Cannon

A heavy-hitting soda cannon that launches pressurized Coke bottles at armored Garbage Trucks. Slower rate of fire, massive damage.

🏎️

Go-Kart Bandits

Fast-moving, lightly armored raiders who zip down the country roads. Quick reflexes — and fast towers — are key to stopping them.

🚛

Garbage Trucks

Slow but armored behemoths that absorb huge amounts of damage before going down. Save your Coke Cannons for these bruisers.

🌊

Wave-Based Gameplay

Survive endless waves of increasingly difficult enemies. Tap the Start Wave button when you're ready and brace for impact.

🪙

Coin Economy

Earn coins for every enemy you defeat. Go-Kart Bandits drop 10 coins; Garbage Trucks drop 20. Spend wisely to build your defenses.

📱

iPhone & iPad

Built with SpriteKit and optimized for both iPhone and iPad. Play in landscape for the best compound-wide view.

How to Play

Tap a white circle to build a tower at a cousin's house Choose Aunt Emma's Fireworks ($50) or Aunt Junita's Coke Cannon ($100) Earn coins by defeating enemies in each wave Cover both roads before tapping Start Wave Don't let enemies reach the Big House!
