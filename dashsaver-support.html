DashSaver — Clocks, Weather & Live Stocks

Need help with DashSaver? Browse the common questions below or reach out directly — we typically respond within 1–2 business days.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn't the weather showing?

DashSaver needs an internet connection to fetch weather data. Make sure your Roku is connected to Wi-Fi and try restarting the screensaver. If the issue persists, the weather service may be temporarily unavailable — it will retry automatically every 10 minutes.

Why aren't the stock prices updating?

Stock data is refreshed every 10 minutes. Prices are only available during market hours (weekdays, 9:30 AM – 4:00 PM ET). Outside market hours, the last known price will be displayed.

How do I change the cities, time zones, or stock symbols?

Open DashSaver from the Roku home screen (not as a screensaver) and the settings screen will appear. From there you can customize all clocks, weather cities, and stock symbols. Your changes are saved automatically.

How do I set DashSaver as my screensaver?

On your Roku, go to Settings → Screensaver → DashSaver and set it as your active screensaver. You can also configure how long the TV waits before activating it.

My settings reset after a Roku restart — what happened?

Settings are stored in the Roku device registry and should persist across restarts. If a factory reset was performed on the device, settings will be cleared. You can re-enter them from the settings screen.

Is DashSaver free?

Yes. DashSaver is completely free with no subscriptions, no in-app purchases, and no ads.

Which Roku devices are supported?

DashSaver is designed for HD and FHD Roku devices running a current firmware version. It has been tested on Roku streaming sticks and Roku TV models.

Contact Support

If your issue isn't covered above, please reach out and include:

  • Your Roku model and firmware version (found in Settings → System → About)
  • A description of what you expected to happen vs. what actually happened
  • Any error messages displayed on screen
